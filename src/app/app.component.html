Help & Documentation

To order a specialty pizza, please navigate to the Home page (by clicking "Home" from the header navigation bar), and scroll down and see our menu of available specialty pizzas.
Important Notes
  • We don't currently allow editing of specialty pizzas
  • You will have to delete your existing pizza and select the right specialty or you may choose "Create your own pizza"

To order a custom pizza, please click on "Create your own pizza". Once at the creation page, please select your pizza crust, sauce and toppings. Currently toppings are unlimited, with maximum quantity of 2 for each. Once your pizza is complete, please add it to the cart via the "Add to cart" button.
Important Notes
  • Pizzas are required to have a crust, sauce and at least 1 topping
  • We don't currently allow editing of custom pizzas
  • You will have to delete your existing pizza and start over

For all other questions and concerns, please email us at help@swe632pizzas.com
